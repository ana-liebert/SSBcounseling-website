SPIRIT SOUL BODY COUNSELING

Spirit Soul Body is a counseling service that addresses the whole person, and utilizes a therapeutic approach that promotes spiritual, emotional, and physical well-being. Owned and operated by Elizabeth Cooley, LCSW-R, Spirit Soul Body Counseling Services is designed to help meet the unique needs of individuals, couples, groups, or families who specifically desire counseling that is supportive of a Christian worldview.

Practice Philosophy-

Elizabeth uses an eclectic approach, which incorporates aspects of the following theoretical frameworks: cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), dialectical behavioral therapy (DBT), strengths based perspective, and attachment theory, as seen through the lens of a Christian world-view. The principal focus is spiritual healing and reconciliation with God.
